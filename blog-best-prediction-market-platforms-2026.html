1. Polymarket — Best Overall

Polymarket is the undisputed market leader with the deepest liquidity, widest range of markets, and most active community. Built on Polygon, it uses USDC for settlement and offers near-instant trading.

Pros: Deepest liquidity, fastest execution, most markets, no KYC (non-US), mobile app, strong community

Cons: Not available to US residents, USDC only, no fiat on-ramp, resolution disputes occasionally slow

Best for: Serious traders who want the most liquid markets and tightest spreads. Non-US users only.

2. Kalshi — Best for US Users

Kalshi is the only CFTC-regulated prediction market in the United States. It accepts USD directly via bank transfer and offers a clean, professional interface. Markets focus on economics, politics, and finance.

Pros: Fully regulated, USD deposits, institutional-grade, excellent for political and economic markets

Cons: KYC required, higher fees (~3%), fewer markets than Polymarket, slower to add trending events

Best for: US-based traders who want regulatory protection and prefer USD settlement.

Drift Protocol runs on Solana and offers prediction markets alongside perpetual futures. Fast settlement, low fees, and integration with the broader Solana DeFi ecosystem.

Prediction Market Strategy Tips

  1. Start small: Risk no more than 5% of your bankroll on any single market
  2. Diversify: Spread across 10-20 uncorrelated markets for consistent returns
  3. Watch for mispricing: Breaking news creates brief windows where prices haven't adjusted
  4. Trade the exit: You don't need to hold until resolution — take profits when probabilities move in your favor
  5. Read resolution criteria carefully: Ambiguous resolution rules are the biggest source of losses
